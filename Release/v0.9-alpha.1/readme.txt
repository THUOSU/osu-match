osu! match
----

Usage:

0.  Find the mp3 to match. Prepare your High-Quality audio file.
1.  Run cmd.exe and excute the following commands.

osumatch.exe "HighQualityAudio.wav" "BeatmapAudio.mp3" "MatchAudio.wav"

2.  Backup "BeatmapAudio.mp3" in your beatmap directory.
3.  Rename "MatchAudio.wav" to "BeatmapAudio.mp3", and move it into your beatmap directory.

Support HQ audio file formats: FLAC, WAV
